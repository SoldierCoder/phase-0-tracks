Unlearn!

Unlearn is a philosophy that is embraced by the Maker Community. This can be demonstrated in several crazy projects including:

  • Homemade Hoverbike
  • Jet Kart

Top reasons why you should not build any of the projects listed above:

  1. It will scare your mother!
  2. 40 million people will see the video of your death if something goes horribly-wrong.
  3. Auto-manufacturers will put a contract out on you if Hover-bike takes off!
